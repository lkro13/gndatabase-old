Summary

He is an adeptus, under the name Alatus, and the only remaining member of the five foremost Yakshas dispatched by Morax to subdue the demonic spirits that plagued Liyue. He currently resides in Wangshu Inn and secludes himself from crowds and human interactions. The name "Xiao" was bestowed unto him by Morax when the archon liberated him from a god that enslaved him.

Introduction

One of the mighty and illuminated adepti guarding Liyue, also heralded as the "Vigilant Yaksha." Despite his youthful appearance, tales of his exploits have been documented for millennia. He is especially fond of Wangshu Inn's Almond Tofu. This is because it tastes just like the sweet dreams he used to devour.
